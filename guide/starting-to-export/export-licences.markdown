You may need a licence before you can export some types of goods. These are called ‘restricted goods’ and they’re:

- [Arms, military or dual use goods and services](https://govuk-import-export.herokuapp.com/guidance/get-a-licence-to-export-arms-military-or-dual-use-goods-and-services.html)
- [Artworks, antiques and culturally significant goods](https://govuk-import-export.herokuapp.com/guidance/get-a-licence-to-export-artworks-antiques-and-culturally-significant-goods.html)
- [CFCs and ozone depleting gases](https://govuk-import-export.herokuapp.com/guidance/get-a-licence-to-export-cfcs-and-ozone-depleting-gases.html)
- [Chemicals](https://govuk-import-export.herokuapp.com/guidance/get-a-licence-to-export-chemicals.html)
- [Circus, endangered, laboratory and research animals](https://govuk-import-export.herokuapp.com/guidance/get-a-licence-to-export-circus-endangered-research-animals.html)
- [Diamonds](https://govuk-import-export.herokuapp.com/guidance/get-a-licence-to-export-diamonds.html)
- [Drugs and medicines](https://govuk-import-export.herokuapp.com/guidance/get-a-licence-to-export-drugs-and-medicines.html)
- [Energy related goods, technology and services](https://govuk-import-export.herokuapp.com/guidance/get-a-licence-to-export-energy-related-goods-technology-and-services.html)
- [Fish and shellfish](https://govuk-import-export.herokuapp.com/guidance/get-a-licence-to-export-fish-shellfish.html)
- [Food and agricultural products](https://govuk-import-export.herokuapp.com/guidance/get-a-licence-to-export-food-and-agricultural-products.html)
- [Furs, skins and other animal products](https://govuk-import-export.herokuapp.com/guidance/get-a-licence-to-export-furs-skins-and-other-animal-products.html)
- [Horses and ponies](https://govuk-import-export.herokuapp.com/guidance/get-a-licence-to-export-horses-ponies.html)
- [Law enforcement and torture goods](https://govuk-import-export.herokuapp.com/guidance/get-a-licence-to-export-law-enforcement-and-torture-goods.html)
- [Livestock and poultry](https://govuk-import-export.herokuapp.com/guidance/get-a-licence-to-export-livestock-poultry.html)
- [Radioactive substances](https://govuk-import-export.herokuapp.com/guidance/get-a-licence-to-export-radioactive-substances.html)
- [Waste](https://govuk-import-export.herokuapp.com/guidance/get-a-licence-to-export-waste.html)
